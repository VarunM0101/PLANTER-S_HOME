About Us

Our story, mission, and vision

Our Story

Planter's Home was founded on February 18, 2025, in Rajender Nagar, Dehradun by Vinayak Manchanda. What started as a passion project has grown into a thriving business that celebrates the beauty of plants and planters as essential elements in home decor.

We believe in elevating planters and plants from mere gardening items to stunning showpiece elements that transform any space. Our journey began with a simple love for gardening and has evolved into a mission to bring the beauty of nature into every home.

Our Mission

To make plants and planters essential elements of home decoration, promoting a greener lifestyle while providing high-quality, aesthetically pleasing products that bring nature's beauty indoors.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of eco-conscious planters and plants, inspiring a generation to embrace the "Go Green, Live Green" philosophy in their homes and lives.

Our Values

Eco-Consciousness

We prioritize sustainable practices and eco-friendly products to minimize our environmental footprint.

Passion

Our love for plants and nature drives everything we do, from product selection to customer service.

Quality

We never compromise on the quality of our products, ensuring they meet the highest standards.

Community

We believe in building a community of plant lovers who share our vision for a greener world.

Meet Our Founder

Vinayak Manchanda

Founder & CEO

Vinayak's journey with plants began as a childhood hobby that grew into a lifelong passion. After years of personal gardening experience, he decided to turn his knowledge and love for plants into a business that could help others discover the joy of living with greenery.

With a vision to make planters and plants standard showpiece items in homes across India, Vinayak founded Planter's Home in February 2025. His dedication to quality, sustainability, and beautiful design guides every aspect of the business.
