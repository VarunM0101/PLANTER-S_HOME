Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver plants and products?

Yes, we offer delivery services in Dehradun and surrounding areas. For larger plants or bulk orders, additional delivery charges may apply. Please contact us for more information.

What if my plant arrives damaged?

We take great care in packaging our plants for delivery. However, if your plant arrives damaged, please contact us within 24 hours with photos, and we'll arrange for a replacement or refund.

Do you provide planting and maintenance services?

Yes, we offer planting assistance and can provide guidance on plant maintenance. For larger projects or regular maintenance, we can customize a service package to meet your needs.

Can I return products?

Non-plant items can be returned within 7 days of purchase if they are in original condition. Plants are non-returnable due to their perishable nature, but we'll assist with any issues related to plant health.
